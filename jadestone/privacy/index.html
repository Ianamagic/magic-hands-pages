Privacy Policy

Jadestone Construction · Effective June 27, 2026

Who we are

Jadestone Construction ("Jadestone", "we", "us") provides handyman and home remodeling services in Brevard County, Florida and surrounding areas. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us: name, phone number, email, and service address.
  • Project details needed to prepare your estimate (type of repair or remodel, scope, scheduling notes).
  • Communication records such as texts, emails, and call notes related to your project.

We collect this information when you contact us by phone, text, our website, or through a Facebook or Instagram lead form.

How we use your information

  • To contact you and prepare a free estimate for your project.
  • To schedule, confirm, and provide your handyman or remodeling service.
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and review requests.
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.

Mobile information & SMS

By providing your phone number, you consent to be contacted by Jadestone Construction by phone call and SMS text message about your project (estimates, confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups). Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help.

We will not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in, or consent with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with trusted service providers that help us operate (for example, scheduling or messaging tools), and only as needed to deliver your service or as required by law. Mobile/SMS consent data is never shared for marketing purposes.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations, and we take reasonable steps to protect it.

Your choices

You can opt out of text messages anytime by replying STOP. You may ask us to update or delete your information by contacting us below.
