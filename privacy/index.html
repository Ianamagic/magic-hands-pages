Who we are

Magic Hands Services LLC ("Magic Hands", "we", "us") provides residential cleaning services in Palm Bay and Brevard County, Florida. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us: name, phone number, email, and service address.
  • Service details needed to plan your cleaning (home size, pets, scheduling notes).
  • Communication records such as texts, emails, and call notes related to your service.

We collect this information when you contact us by phone, text, our website, or through a Google Local Services request.

How we use your information

  • To schedule, confirm, and provide your cleaning service.
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and review requests.
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.

Mobile information & SMS

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Magic Hands Services LLC about your service (assessments, confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and review requests). Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help.

We will not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in, or consent with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with trusted service providers that help us operate (for example, scheduling, payment, or messaging tools), and only as needed to deliver your service or as required by law. As stated above, mobile/SMS consent data is never shared for marketing purposes.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations, and we take reasonable steps to protect it.

Your choices

You can opt out of text messages anytime by replying STOP. You may ask us to update or delete your information by contacting us below.
